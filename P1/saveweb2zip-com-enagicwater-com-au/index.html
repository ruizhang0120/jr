Why Enagic?

How does the Kangen K8 Water Machine actually work?

The K8 takes your regular tap water, removes bacteria and pollutants and transforms the water into Electrolysed Reduced Water (ERW) with over 60 uses.

ERW is produced through electrolysis.

The water molecules are structurally changed creating H2 – a free radical scavenger and powerful antioxidant.

This supports your body to function at an optimum level.
